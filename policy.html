Rules & Policies

  • There is a $10 non refundable deposit due at the time of booking. This deposit is a seat holding deposit. All deposits can be sent to CashApp ($theellises4) or Venmo (@kjs-nail-hause).
  • After hour appointments may be available. There is a $15 fee added to your service in order to book these times. Please text (309)706-7651 to check availability.
  • There is a 15 MINUTE grace period. After that, your appointment is cancelled.
  • Let me know if you need a soak off at the time of booking. My appointments are time sensitive and soak offs are an added time.
  • No kids or extra guests at the time of your appointment.
  • Please wear a mask at ALL times.
  • For safety reasons, I do not soak off other techs work. Please come with bare nails unless you are getting a fill over my work.
  • Please make sure to use the restroom before you arrive. Bathroom access will be limited due to covid-19.
  • I DO NOT issue refunds. There is a three(3) day grace period for any breaks or lifting on your nails. After that you will be charged for repairs.
  • If your fill requires more than 3 nails being repaired, you will be charged for a full set.
  • Cashapp, cash and Venmo are accepted forms of payment but please have exact change if you choose to pay cash.
  • If you cancel your appointment less than 24 hours before your time slot, you will lose your deposit and be banned from booking.
  • The nail room is cleaned between each client and the end of each day to prevent the spread of COVID-19. KJ's Nail Hause and all other entities related to are not responsible for catching or spreading COVID-19 and will not be held responsible if you do contract the virus.
  • You are only allowed to rescedule twice(2) under the same deposit. After that, your deposit is forfeited and a new deposit is required for booking.
