Rules & Policies

IMPORTANT: READ BEFORE CONTINUING!

  • There is a $10 non refundable deposit due at the time of booking. This goes towards the cost of the service. All deposits can be sent to CashApp ($theellises4) or Venmo (@kjs-nail-hause).
  • I am only accepting cash for the full service for the time being. Please be prepared for cash payment of services.
  • If you arrive 5 minutes after your appointment, you will be charged a $5 late fee.
  • There is a 10 MINUTE grace period. After that, your appointment is cancelled.

  • After hour appointments may be available. There is a $15 fee added to your service in order to book these times. Please text (309)706-7651 to check availability.
  • Let me know if you need a soak off at the time of booking. My appointments are time sensitive and soak offs are an added time.

  • Please come with bare nails unless you are getting a fill over my work.

  • Cashapp, cash and Venmo are accepted forms of payment but please have exact change if you choose to pay cash.
  • No call no shows will lose their deposit and be banned from booking.

  • You are only allowed to rescedule once(1) under the same deposit. After that, your deposit is forfeited and a new deposit is required for booking.
