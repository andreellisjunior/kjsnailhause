Who Am I

My Name is Kaitlynn

Thank you for choosing me to be your nail technician! I live in Bloomington Illinois. I started doing nails in March of 2020. Before nails, I did hair for 2 years. I’ve always been artistic, so it was only a matter of time before I discovered my passion, which is nails. My specialties are wild designs and encapsulation. I’m perfecting my craft everyday and I’m so glad to have you with me on this fun journey!
